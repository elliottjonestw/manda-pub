New18 July 2026

Achievements

Your studying now earns achievements — 320 badges across twelve tracks, all quietly worked out from what you already do. Words known, words mastered, review streaks, active days, lessons finished, texts read and more each have their own ladder to climb.

There's nothing new to grind and no points to chase: a badge is earned the moment your real progress passes its mark. That also means restoring a backup or syncing another device lights up everything you'd already earned — there's no separate score to keep.

A new Achievements card on your Dashboard shows the badges you've earned most recently and the next few within reach. From there, View all achievements opens the full Achievements page — every badge, ordered by how close you are, with the ones you've already earned up top.
